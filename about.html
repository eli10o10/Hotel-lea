About Hotel LEA

Lea's hotel is a family hotel that has been established since 2020 It has been a hotel run by Eli's family. The purpose of the hotel is to make life comfortable for people especially business men and women who travel around the worls. it is also there for leisure and relaxation. All LEA's hotel seek is the comfort of their customers. It has four branches which are located in Argentina, Brazil, Greece and Ghana.
